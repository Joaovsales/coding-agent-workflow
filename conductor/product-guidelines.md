# Product Guidelines: PDF Idea Generator v2

## 1. Brand Messaging & Tone

The communication for "PDF Idea Generator v2" will be **Creative & Inspiring**, focusing on fostering innovation and new content ideas. The tone will encourage users to explore their creativity and discover new possibilities for content generation.

## 2. Visual Identity

The visual identity of the application will be **Modern & Minimalist**. This will translate into a clean, sleek design with an emphasis on usability, ensuring a streamlined and aesthetically pleasing user experience.

## 3. User Interaction

The interaction style will be **Unobtrusive & Empowering**. The interface will be designed to allow users to explore and discover features on their own, providing a seamless and intuitive experience without excessive hand-holding.

## 4. Feedback Mechanism for AI-Generated Content

For AI-generated content, the primary feedback mechanism will be **Direct Editing & Customization**. Users will be empowered to directly modify and fine-tune AI-generated TikTok scripts and ideas to perfectly align with their creative vision.

## 5. Error Handling

In the event of errors or unexpected behaviors, the project will prioritize **Graceful Degradation**. This means ensuring that core functionality remains available and the user experience is not severely disrupted, even when certain features or modules encounter issues. Users will be informed of problems without being blocked from essential tasks.
